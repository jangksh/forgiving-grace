Our Story

Born from Grace,
Worn with Purpose

Forgiving Grace was born from a simple belief — that faith should be lived out loud, not kept in the pews. We wanted to create clothing that carries meaning beyond fashion, pieces that remind you — and everyone around you — of God's unconditional grace.

Genesis 1:28 reminds us that we are made in His image, blessed and called with purpose. Every thread in our garments is a prayer, every design a testament. We don't just make clothes — we make declarations.

This is more than a brand. It's a movement of faith expressed through style.
